NiShiKi : Frequently Asked Questions

A list of often asked questions and tips

I found a bug in NiShiKi source code, what should I do?

Thank you for finding a bug! Please open a new issue in the issue page of the GitHub repository. If you are not familier with GitHub, you can email to the author directly. The author's contact address is written in the README.
